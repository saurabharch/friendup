Notice:

Friend Network Power Sharing is under heavy development and can be considered as a prototype and pre-beta feature. Please keep this in mind. Security is the security of a prototype, not reviewed yet by security experts. When you share your power, some code is ran on your machine in a secure tunnel of iFrames and browser-to-browser encryption. It has no access to any part of your Friend machine, it saves and loads its data directly on the machine of the user asking for power through Friend Network. Despite of that, we suggest that you de-activate this option after having tested it with your personal devices and the ones of your friends. Have fun discovering your new super-computer: the world!
